. ABOUT

Gregg was born and raised in Brooklyn New York. Gregg perfected his craft at the renowned Terry Schrieber Theater.

He has been acting since 2008 and has appeared on various television series including : WHITE COLLAR, DECEPTION, THE AMERICANS, and POWER, to name a few.

Gregg has also starred in several independent movies in the last few years including: SECOND CHANCE, BEHIND CLOSED DOORS, AND BEREAVEMENT.
